In Extraterrestrials you are the Earth's last line of defense from the oncoming alien horde. How long can you hold them back? The small aliens are worth 30 points, the medium ones are 20, the large ones 10, and the red ones between 50 and 250. Fly carefully, as you only gain an extra life every 1500 points!
