In Meteoroids, you are in control of a spaceship, and meteors are flying everywhere. How long will you survive? Big meteors are worth 20 points, medium ones are 50, and small ones are 100. You gain an extra life every 10000 points and an extra hyperspace every 5 waves. Your score is saved automatically in your browser, so you can start again at any time!
